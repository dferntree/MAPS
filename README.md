# MAPS (My Academic Planning System)

## Tired of confusing course catalogs and academic planning stress?

---
### Project Description

**MAPS** is an **all-in-one academic planning platform** that turns the entire Queens College CS major into an **interactive, gamified, and data-driven experience**.

This platform is designed to:
* **Enable Students to Understand Their Degree:** Provides a clear, manageable path through complex degree requirements.
* **Help You Choose the Right Classes:** Offers data-driven insights and reviews to pick the best sections.
* **Find the Best Professors:** Integrates professor reviews and ratings to guide selection.
* **Build Your Perfect Schedule:** Allows you to effortlessly build an optimized academic schedule from day one to graduation.
